makesweird.art

this is a reminder to make weird art!

it doesn't have to be good. it doesn't have to make sense. it doesn't even have to be finished, it just has to exist.

your streak counts how many days in a row you've shown up. miss two and it resets.

one secret key. no email. no password. we store your streak, username, and nothing else.

this site is made by hand, and tries to stay under ~10kb.

it's also open source.

now go make something.
